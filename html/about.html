About Us

Ciseco is a new startup that specializes in designing and manufacturing clothing for men. The company aims to offer high-quality, stylish clothing at an affordable price point. Their product line includes a variety of men's clothing items such as t-shirts, button-down shirts, pants, shorts, and jackets.
